About


Since 2016, the Pongowl Animation Project has pioneered an ambitious yet homemade style of filmmaking. This is my showcase of brickfilms, brought to life with stop-motion animation.

Through compelling and unique visuals, I tell stories in an effort to explore human nature and create wholesome entertainment for all ages.
